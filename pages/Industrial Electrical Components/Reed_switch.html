What is a Reed Switch?

A reed switch is an electrical switch operated by a magnetic field.

It consists of thin metal reeds sealed inside a glass tube. When a magnet comes close, the reeds attract each other and the circuit closes.

  • It is a contactless switch in the sense that no mechanical actuator is needed; a magnetic field operates it.
  • Commonly used in security systems, sensors, and relays.

Components of a Reed Switch

  1. Glass Tube
    • Transparent enclosure
    • Protects the internal contacts from dust, moisture, and mechanical stress
  2. Reed Contacts
    • Thin, flexible metal strips (usually ferromagnetic)
    • The contacts touch each other when a magnetic field is present
  3. Leads / Terminals
    • Metal wires extending from both ends
    • Connect the switch to an external circuit
  4. Inert Gas (Optional)
    • Some reed switches are filled with nitrogen or argon
    • Prevents oxidation and improves reliability

Working of a Reed Switch

  1. A magnet is brought near the reed switch.
  2. The magnetic field magnetizes the reeds, causing them to attract each other.
  3. Contacts close the circuit → current flows (Normally Open type)
  4. When the magnet is removed, the reeds return to their original position, opening the circuit.

Logic:

  • Normally Open (NO) → Closed by magnetic field
  • Normally Closed (NC) → Opened by magnetic field

Types of Reed Switch

  1. Normally Open (NO)
    • Contacts are separated when no magnet is present
    • Closes when a magnet approaches
  2. Normally Closed (NC)
    • Contacts are together when no magnet is present
    • Opens when a magnet approaches
  3. Form A, B, C Classification
    • Form A: SPST (Single Pole Single Throw, NO)
    • Form B: SPST (NC)
    • Form C: SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw)

Applications of Reed Switch

  • Security Systems : Door/window sensors ,Burglar alarms
  • Proximity Sensing : Detecting object presence without physical contact ,E.g., robotic arms, machinery.
  • Consumer Electronics :Mobile phones (flip phones detect cover open/close),Laptops (lid close detection).
  • Industrial and Automotive :Speed sensing ,Position sensing ,Level detection (fluid level sensors).
  • Relays and Telecommunication: Acts as a switching element in reed relays.
