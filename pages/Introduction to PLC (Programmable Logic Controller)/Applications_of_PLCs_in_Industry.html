Application

PLCs are widely used in various industrial and commercial applications, including:

  1. Manufacturing and Assembly Lines: control of conveyors, robotic arms, and packaging systems.
  2. Process Industries: chemical plants, oil and gas, water treatment systems.
  3. Power Plants: control of turbines, boilers, and substations.
  4. Automobile Industry: automated welding, painting, and testing systems.
  5. Building Automation: HVAC systems, elevators, lighting control.
  6. Food and Beverage Industry: bottling, filling, and labeling processes.
